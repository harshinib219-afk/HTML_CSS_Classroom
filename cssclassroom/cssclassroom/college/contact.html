Government Degree College for Women,Begumpet details

Government Degree College for Women (GDCW), Begumpet, established on August 21, 1971, is affiliated with Osmania University and holds NAAC A+ accreditation valid until August 29, 2027. It offers undergraduate courses in arts, commerce, science, and management streams, focusing on women's education in a secular environment. Key facilities include hostels for outstation students, well-equipped labs, a library, sports areas, and spaces for cultural and extracurricular activities
