About Our College

Welcome to Government Degree College for Women, Begumpet(Autonomous)! We are committed to providing quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment for our students.

Our college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. We have a dedicated faculty that is passionate about teaching and research, ensuring that our students receive the best education possible.

We also have state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, a modern library, and comfortable classrooms. Our campus is designed to promote a vibrant academic and social life for our students.


History of GDCW, Begumpet(Autonomous)

Established in 1971 , Government Degree College for Women, Begumpet (GDCW Begumpet), is a premier, autonomous, and NAAC A+ accredited institution in Hyderabad, Telangana. It offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate courses, serving women students with a focus on empowerment through knowledge.


Location: Centrally located in Begumpet, Hyderabad, opposite the Hyderabad Public School, making it accessible via various transport modes.
Affiliation: Affiliated with Osmania University.
